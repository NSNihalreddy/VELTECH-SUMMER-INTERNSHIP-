About Customer Churn Prediction System

The Customer Churn Prediction System is a Machine Learning-based web application developed during the Innolift Ventures Internship Program. The project predicts whether a customer is likely to leave the company's services (Churn) or continue using them (No Churn). The system combines a trained Logistic Regression model, Flask backend, SQLite database, and a responsive user interface to provide real-time predictions, customer record management, and business insights through an interactive dashboard.

Prediction Field Description
Customer ID Unique identifier assigned to each customer.
Age Age of the customer in years.
Tenure Months Duration the customer has been associated with the company.
Average Order Value Average amount spent by the customer per order.
Total Orders Total number of orders placed by the customer.
Last Purchase Days Ago Number of days since the customer's most recent purchase.
Support Tickets Total support requests or complaints raised by the customer.
Gender Customer demographic information used during prediction.
City Customer location information.
Subscription Type Membership plan selected by the customer (Gold, Platinum, or Silver).
Prediction Result Indicates whether the customer is likely to Churn or remain active.
Confidence Score Shows the confidence level of the Machine Learning model prediction.
